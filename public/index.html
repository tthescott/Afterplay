What is Afterplay?

Do you like when two songs have seamless transitions between them? Do you hate having to open Spotify to queue the next song when the first one plays? Then Afterplay is for you!


When you pair two songs, Afterplay automatically queues the second song when the first one plays, on two conditions:


1) Shuffle is on (so it doesn't interrupt you listening through an album or playlist in order)
2) You have added no songs to the queue (so it doesn't interrupt your queue)

Pair two songs to get started!
